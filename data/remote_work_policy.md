# Remote Work Standard

**Version:** 2.1
**Effective Date:** 2023-06-15
**Department:** Human Resources

## 1. Eligibility

Remote work is available to employees whose roles are compatible with off-site work, subject to manager approval.

## 2. Equipment & Security

- **Company Devices:** Employees must use company-issued laptops for all work-related tasks. Personal devices are strictly prohibited for accessing internal networks.
- **VPN:** Always connect via the corporate VPN when accessing internal resources from public Wi-Fi.
- **Physical Security:** Lock your screen when stepping away. Ensure your workspace is private and screens are not visible to unauthorized persons.

## 3. Availability

- **Core Hours:** Remote employees must be available during core business hours (10:00 AM - 3:00 PM local time).
- **Communication:** Employees are expected to respond to Slack messages and emails within a reasonable timeframe during work hours.

## 4. Expenses

- **Stipend:** The company provides a one-time $500 stipend for home office setup.
- **Internet:** Monthly internet costs are not reimbursed unless explicitly included in the employment contract.
