# Data Classification Standard

**Version:** 3.0
**Effective Date:** 2023-09-01
**Department:** Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC)

## 1. Classification Levels

### Level 1: Public

Information intended for public release (e.g., marketing materials, press releases).
*Handling:* No restrictions.

### Level 2: Internal

Information for internal business use (e.g., org charts, policies, routine emails).
*Handling:* Do not share outside the company without authorization.

### Level 3: Confidential

Sensitive information that could cause harm if disclosed (e.g., pricing strategies, non-public financial results).
*Handling:* Encryption required in transit. Access limited to need-to-know.

### Level 4: Restricted (High Risk)

Highly sensitive data including PII, PHI, and credentials.
*Handling:* Encryption required at rest and in transit. Strict logging of all access. MFA required for access.

## 2. Labeling

All documents and emails containing Level 3 or 4 data must be clearly labeled in the header or subject line.

## 3. Data Retention

- Level 1 & 2: Retain as business needs dictate.
- Level 3 & 4: Retain for 7 years or as required by regulation, then securely destroy.
