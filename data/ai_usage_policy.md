# Global AI Usage Policy

**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** 2024-01-01
**Department:** IT Security

## 1. Purpose

This policy outlines the acceptable use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) tools, including Generative AI (e.g., ChatGPT, Claude, GitHub Copilot), within the company.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, and third parties accessing company systems.

## 3. Acceptable Use

- **Approved Tools:** Only AI tools vetted and approved by IT Security may be used for company business. Current approved list: GitHub Copilot (Enterprise), ChatGPT Enterprise.
- **Code Generation:** AI-generated code must be reviewed by a human peer and scanned for security vulnerabilities before deployment.
- **Content Creation:** AI may be used to draft emails, reports, and marketing copy, provided the output is reviewed for accuracy and tone.

## 4. Prohibited Use

- **Sensitive Data:** DO NOT input Personally Identifiable Information (PII), Protected Health Information (PHI), or confidential intellectual property into public AI models.
- **Decision Making:** AI tools must not be the sole decision-maker for hiring, performance reviews, or legal determinations.
- **Copyright:** Do not claim AI-generated content as original human work where prohibited by law or contract.

## 5. Violation

Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination.
